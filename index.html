ABOUT US


Kashiyatra, the annual socio-cultural fest of IIT(BHU) Varanasi is known to witness numerous artists flying from worldwide and becomes home for participants from throughout the country. Kashiyatra is not just a fest but an emotion, an expression of euphoria. A stage celebrating art form, a podium igniting intellectual thoughts,kashiyatra has evolved to be a platform for global cultural interaction over the years. It has lived through decades of musical and cultural change, not only keeping up with the times but setting new standards each year.
